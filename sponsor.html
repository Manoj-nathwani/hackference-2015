Supporting Hackference

Hackference is coming around again and we want to make it as good as it can be for our attendees!

Not only do we want to provide a fantastic conference at a reasonable price, but we want to make another fantastic free hackathon as well

Other the course of the three days, sponsors will be in front of over 300 developers from all over the UK and some from further a field.

We're lucky at Hackference to have a diverse mixture of attendees, previous event's have had plenty of student, startup, corporate and freelance attendees from all over the skill spectrum

If you'd like to discuss sponsoring Hackference, please contact sponsorship@hackference.co.uk
