Supporting Hackference

Hackference is coming around again and we want to make it as good as it can be for our attendees! And to do that we need your help with the conference and hackathon.

The Basics

Date

Friday 2nd October, 2015 to Sunday 4th October, 2015

Attendees

Up to 400 over the course of the 3 days, with ~250 at the conference and ~150 at the hackathon.

Format

A single day, multi-track conference followed by a 24 hour hackathon.

About Hackference

Hackference has always been about learning, providing a conference with material to help people learn and to inspire people to build with different technologies.

The hackathon will always be a natural extension, providing a place for hackers to try technology, surrounded by like minded people and experts all willing to help each other learn

We're lucky at Hackference to have a diverse mixture of attendees, previous event's have had plenty of student, startup, corporate and freelance attendees from all over the skill spectrum.

If you'd like to discuss sponsoring Hackference, please contact sponsorship@hackference.co.uk
