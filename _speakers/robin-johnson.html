A little about Robin...

A Polyglot Programmer and Developer Evangelist at SendGrid, currently working amongst the world-wide developer community to ease the adoption of Email API technology. Having spoken at some of the world's leading technical conferences, Robin is building his way to being a key player in the London tech scene; attending, speaking at, and even helping organise multiple large events. Mentoring on topics such as Distributed Systems, Golang, NoSQL Databases and Open Source values; Robin is as much hacker as he is hustler.
