A little about Hugh...

Having recently finished a degree in Music Computing from Goldsmiths, University of London, Hugh is an Android Developer at Soundwave Analytics Ltd, and a contributor to Meyda, a javascript web audio feature extraction library, as well as the node-web-audio-api. Interests include Music Information Retrieval, computational musical performance, and ephemeral audio technology.
